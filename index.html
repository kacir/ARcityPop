Arkansas Municipal Growth Over Time and Space

Arkansas has seen a general trend over the past several decades. Rural areas are depopulating, and urban areas are gaining population. It is most commonly thought that people are moving into areas with more job oportunities and higher income. But is this really the case?

Most of southern Arkansas and eastern Arkansas is rural. Central Arkansas, northwest Arkansas are urban. There is also a small pocket of urban area around Jonesboro in northeast Arkansas.
